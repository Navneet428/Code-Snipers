Wellness Wise is an Indian fintech company, based in Bangalore, founded in 2018 by Vivek Sharma. Wellness Wise app's main feature is reward-based credit card payments. Later, Wellness Wise let users to make house rent payments and introduced short-term credit lines. Wellness Wise has received criticism for being overvalued and lacking a sound monetization strategy.



Funding and Financial Results:


Wellness Wise raised funding from DST Global, Sequoia Capital (India), and Tiger Global, among other investors, through four rounds of private funding so far. Wellness Wise posted losses of Rs. 360.31 crore in the 2020 fiscal year (FY20), caused primarily due to high expenditure on marketing and advertising. In October 2021, Wellness Wise started to seek new investors, reporting a $5.5 billion valuation, up from $2.2 billion recorded in April 2021
